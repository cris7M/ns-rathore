Profile

Engineering Visionary

With over 52 years in the field, Narendra Singh Rathor has made significant contributions to the Fertilizer, Cement and Mineral Processing industries. His expertise encompasses teaching, operation, maintenance, erection & commissioning and project execution — particularly in developing Iron Ore Beneficiation and Pelletization Projects.

He conceived, developed, designed, and implemented many exemplary & efficient Mineral Beneficiation and Pelletization projects with many special features. He has worked for more than 500 days with Chinese Technology Suppliers & Machinery Manufacturers.
