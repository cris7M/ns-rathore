About N.S. Rathor
Consulting

An expert solutions provider for optimized mineral processing and engineering — pioneering India's beneficiation landscape since 2005.

Who We Are

Two Decades of
Pioneering Innovation

N. S. Rathor Consultant specializes in providing innovative engineering solutions, specifically in Mineral Beneficiation & Pelletization. Our expertise lies in enhancing productivity with tailored consulting and training programs.

Since 2005, we have been at the forefront of engineering consultancy, process engineering, and plant design in the mineral processing industry. Our expertise spanning Iron Ore, Manganese Ore, Coal, Silica Sand, Kaolin Clay, and more — we offer end-to-end consultancy services from concept to commissioning.

Backed by decades of experience and industry-leading innovations, we have successfully implemented cutting-edge technologies across mineral processing plants, setting new benchmarks in performance, efficiency, and sustainability.

Our team is led by a highly accomplished technologist with over 50+ years of diverse experience, including:

  • Beneficiation of Iron, Manganese, Coal, Quartz, Silica Sand, Kaolin Clay etc.
  • Iron Ore Pelletization across multiple technologies and development of advanced techniques
  • Erection, commissioning of plant & machineries
  • Execution of large-scale industrial projects (fertilizer & cement)
  • Training and development of plant personnel

Landmark Achievements

First of Its Kind Innovations

We are recognized as pioneers in introducing first-of-its-kind Beneficiation and Pelletization technologies in India.

🏭
India's First Low-Grade Iron Ore Plant
Designed and commissioned India's first Low-Grade Iron Ore Beneficiation Plant at Eurobond Industries (now 4MANN), Jabalpur — a landmark that changed the industry.
💧
First Slime Beneficiation Plant
Designed and commissioned India's first Iron Ore Slime Beneficiation Plant for SAIL, Dalli Rajhara
🔧
Introduced Filter Press Concept for Tailing De-watering Application
Introduced Filter Press concept for De-watering of Iron ore tailing and reducing specific water consumption from 1.2 cum/T to <0. 2cu.m/T.
🔥
Flash Roasting Magnetising
Pioneered Iron Ore Beneficiation process based on Flash Roasting Magnetising Technology — one of the most advanced approaches to challenging ore types.
🧲
High-Intensity Magnetic Separators
Introduced High-Intensity Permanent Magnet Plate type separator in Iron and Manganese Ore beneficiation for coarse to fine particles from 6mm to 10 microns.
🌏
Global Technology Expertise
Over 500 days spent in China evaluating technology suppliers and machinery manufacturers — ensuring only the best-in-class equipment is recommended for every project.
🔬
Vacuum Ceramic Disc Filters
Introduced Vacuum Ceramic Disc filters for Sub-65% Fe Iron Ore concentrate, achieving below 9% moisture — a breakthrough that dramatically improved concentrate quality.
