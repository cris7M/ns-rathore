Profile

Engineering Visionary

With over 52 years in the field, Narendra Singh Rathor has made significant contributions to the Fertilizer, Cement and Mineral Processing industries. His expertise encompasses teaching, operation, maintenance, erection & commissioning and project execution — particularly in developing Iron Ore Beneficiation and Pelletization Projects.

He conceived, developed, designed, and implemented many exemplary & efficient Iron Ore, Manganese Ore, Silica Sand, Kaolin Clay Beneficiation and Pelletization projects with many special features. He has spent more than 500 days in China evaluating large numbers of Technology Suppliers & Machinery Manufacturers.

Notable Achievements at Gujarat Ambuja Cement

Engineering India's Best Cement Plant

At Gujarat Ambuja Cement's first plant at Kodinar, Gujarat (1984–86), N.S. Rathor developed and implemented an improved Process & Instrumentation Control system using indigenously available hardware — contributing to the highest productivity of man and machinery.

As a result, this plant proved to be the best-performing cement plant in India and among the three best cement plants in the world — creating a history of operating 341 days in a year.
